Why this pivot

A deliberate career shift
in response to a changing market

Tech journalism as I knew it has fundamentally changed. AI generates content faster and cheaper than humans, and the editor's added value has significantly declined. Rather than fighting this trend, I'm applying 20 years of technology knowledge where human expertise still matters: testing, diagnostics, systems administration.

This isn't an experiment or a midlife crisis. It's a strategic decision—leveraging experience in an area that AI won't replace anytime soon. There's no going back to the previous path.
